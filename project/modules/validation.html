Wallet Validation

Validate your wallet securely to solve blockchain-related issues

What is Wallet Validation?

Wallet validation is a process that verifies your wallet's integrity and resolves issues that might prevent it from functioning correctly. Our validation service helps identify and fix problems with:

  • Transaction failures or stuck transactions
  • Missing or unrecognized tokens
  • Connection issues with dApps
  • Synchronization problems with the blockchain
  • Wallet recovery and access issues
Secure Process

Your validation is conducted using completely decentralized methods, ensuring your funds and personal information remain secure throughout the process.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the validation process secure?

Yes, our validation process is completely secure and decentralized. We do not store your private keys or recovery phrases on our servers. The validation is performed client-side using industry-standard encryption.

How long does the validation process take?

The validation process typically takes between 30 seconds to 2 minutes, depending on the complexity of the issue and current blockchain network congestion.

What types of wallets are supported?

We support all major cryptocurrency wallets including MetaMask, Trust Wallet, Coinbase Wallet, Binance Chain Wallet, and any wallet compatible with WalletConnect. If you have a custom wallet, you can select "Other" from the dropdown menu.

What should I do if the validation doesn't fix my issue?

If the validation doesn't resolve your issue, you can try the specific service related to your problem (Rectification, Recovery, etc.) or contact our support team for personalized assistance.

Is there a cost for wallet validation?

Basic validation is free. Some advanced recovery or rectification services may require minimal gas fees for on-chain transactions, but you'll always be informed before any fees are applied.
